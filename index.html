Ocimum international school

OCIMUM is committed to providing exemplary teaching designed to make the child a productive member of the community. The spirit of intellectual curiosity, collaboration, critical thinking and effective communication is fostered in the students.
Instruction is aimed at supporting each student's ability to achieve optimum levels. We support the students and act as collaborative guides in their education. Students are also encouraged in the exploration of their intellectual, artistic, technological, physical, social and character development.
Our rigorous standards based instructional programme is geared towards enabling all students to meet the challenges of the future with confidence and compassion.
